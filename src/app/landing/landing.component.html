How it works

A non-algorithmic way to get Spotify song recommendations

01

Log in with Spotify

Grant this app access to your Spotify account so we can create playlists and add tracks on your behalf.

02

Create a playlist

A new playlist will be created in your Spotify account. You'll receive a unique song-rec.me URL that you can copy from this site or by clicking the "Copy to Clipboard" button next to the playlist name.

03

Share your playlist and start receiving recommendations!

Share your playlist URL with friends. They can search for songs and send them directly to your playlist. You'll receive an email notification whenever someone adds a song.
